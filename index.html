🌆 About DomeCredit City

DomeCredit City is an AI-powered, blockchain-based sustainable city with eco-friendly architecture and smart infrastructure. It is designed to optimize energy efficiency, economic stability, and community well-being.

🏗️ Architecture & District Development

The city is divided into multiple districts, each with a unique focus:

  • Residential Districts – eco-friendly housing with smart home integration.
  • Business Hubs – AI-powered commercial centers and office spaces.
  • Innovation & Tech Zones – research centers for AI, biotech, and sustainability.
  • Green Spaces – urban forests, parks, and solar-powered energy zones.

💰 Financial System & Payment Model

The city operates with a blockchain-based cryptocurrency, **DomeCredit (DMC)**, ensuring transparency and eliminating corruption.

  • Sector-Based Pricing – Fixed pricing on essential goods to prevent inflation.
  • Automatic Upgrades – Residents earning DMC get automatic housing upgrades.
  • Investment Pools – Collected funds are directed toward infrastructure expansion.

🌱 Incentives & Social Benefits

Residents receive benefits based on contribution to the city's ecosystem.

  • Workforce Protection – Special packages for injured workers and disabled individuals.
  • Housing Development – Discounts for young families and large households.
  • Eco-Engagement Rewards – Bonuses for sustainable practices and energy conservation.

🌍 Investment & Expansion Strategy

The city is funded through multiple investment channels:

  • **Global Investors & Smart Funds** – Encouraging private and corporate investments.
  • **Sectoral Development Plans** – Funding allocated based on district expansion.
  • **Real Estate Tokenization** – Digital assets representing property ownership.
